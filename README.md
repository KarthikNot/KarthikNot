## About Me

I'm **Karthikeyan Anumalla** — an Aspiring ML/AI Engineer based in Hyderabad, India.

B.E. in Computer Science Engineering (AI & ML) from Neil Gogte Institute of Technology, Osmania University — CGPA 8.67.

Passionate about building intelligent systems, from training models to deploying production-ready AI solutions.

- Focused on **ML/AI Engineering** — RAG pipelines, LLM integration, and agentic systems
- Experienced in **Python backend development**, data pipelines, and containerised deployments
- Built projects spanning **Agentic RAG, recommendation systems, and full-stack AI applications**
- IBM Data Science Professional Certificate — Coursera, 2024
- Open to **ML / AI / Backend opportunities**
- Interests: Gaming & Exploring Computer Tech
- Based in Hyderabad, Telangana
